Blog Content

Hello and welcome to my blog!

This space doesn’t claim to be anything special—it’s simply a tool for organizing my thoughts, readings, and learnings. As you know, the world of deep learning evolves incredibly quickly. I find it very challenging to effectively process all the information, papers, and posts I come across. I needed a way to gather and manage all these insights efficiently, and that’s why I created this blog.

The posts here don't aspire to be definitive or authoritative. In fact, it’s quite certain that I approach many topics in a rather naive manner, given my limited experience (having graduated just two years ago). I am far from being an expert, and I understand that my understanding of many subjects is still developing.

To facilitate a more collaborative learning process, I’ve added a comment system (Giscus) under each post. I encourage everyone to share their opinions, correct any points, and not hesitate to point out when something is not entirely accurate. Your feedback is invaluable in helping me—and hopefully others—learn and grow in this fascinating field.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you find something useful or thought-provoking here. Let's learn together!
